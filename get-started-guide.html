Getting Started
1

Log in with your email

Enter your email and check your inbox for a 6-digit code. No password needed.

2

Add your first content

Click the yellow "Add Content" button or press N. Fill in the title, type, dates, and assignee.

3

Move cards as work progresses

Drag cards between columns or use the arrow buttons that appear on hover.
