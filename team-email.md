# Email to Team

**Subject:** Your Content Calendar is Live - Here's How to Get Started

---

Hey team,

Our new Content Calendar is live and ready to use. Here's everything you need to know:

**Link:** https://peoples-league-calendar.vercel.app

**Login:** Enter your email and you'll receive a 6-digit code. That's it - no password to remember.

---

### The 60-Second Overview

**Pipeline** - Your main workspace. Cards move left to right as content progresses:
- Shoot Scheduled → In Production → Editing → Review → Scheduled → Published

**Calendar** - See what's shooting and releasing by date

**Ideas** - Drop ideas here. Click "Start" when ready to move one into production.

---

### Quick Actions

- **Add content:** Click the yellow "Add Content" button (top of sidebar) or press **N**
- **Move a card:** Drag it to a new column, or use the arrow buttons on hover
- **Edit a card:** Click on it
- **Undo a mistake:** Watch for the "Undo" toast at the bottom (5 seconds to click it)

---

### Tips

- Use the filters (All / Long-form / Short-form) to focus on what matters to you
- Release dates show in pink on cards so you can spot deadlines fast
- The Calendar view shows "S" for shoot days and "R" for release days

That's it. Jump in and start adding your projects. Reach out if you hit any snags.
